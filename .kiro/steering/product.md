## Core Concept

Build and optimize Santa's automated workshop to produce high-quality toys before Christmas. Players manage resource extraction, processing chains, power grids, and logistics while balancing traditional craftsmanship with modern automation and magical enhancements.

## Key Features

- **Seasonal Cycle**: January → December in-game year with Christmas Rush finale
- **Mission-Based Progression**: Campaign tutorials → infinite sandbox mode
- **Branching Research**: 8 tech trees (Automation, Energy, Materials, Toys, Logistics, Elf Management, Magic, Aesthetics)
- **Quality Grading**: S/A/B/C toy grades based on speed, materials, energy efficiency, and magic infusion
- **No Fail State**: Underperformance yields coaching and softer goals, not game over
- **Prestige System**: Seasonal resets with meta-upgrades and persistent unlocks

## Tone & Aesthetic

Warm, humorous, and encouraging. Nordic festive aesthetic with realistic isometric visuals. Storybook UI with parchment panels and brass frames. Orchestral music with bells and celesta.

## Target Audience

Fans of Factorio, Satisfactory, and cozy management games. Players who enjoy optimization puzzles with a festive, low-stress atmosphere.

## Success Metrics

- Steam rating ≥85%
- Player retention >25% at 30 days
- Average session length 45–90 minutes
- Christmas Spirit Score as primary engagement metric

## Design Pillars

1. **Optimization Without Stress**: Complex systems with forgiving mechanics
2. **Festive Atmosphere**: Warm, cozy aesthetic with holiday charm
3. **Meaningful Progression**: Unlocks that fundamentally change gameplay
4. **Creative Expression**: Multiple valid solutions to production challenges
5. **Seasonal Rhythm**: Natural pacing through the in-game year
